Multi-Agent Testing Framework

A dual-mode testing framework where specialist agents share the work and a human orchestrates. Eight agents in Discovery mode; passing flows graduate to Playwright spec files in Regression mode (zero tokens, runs in CI). Built to explore how AI-assisted testing scales when the work is too varied for any single agent.

Learning: Multi-agent systems can take on complex testing workflows while a human stays in control of the work.

Themes I keep returning to

  • Human oversight becomes more important, not less.

  • AI works best when embedded into existing workflows.
  • Reliability is often an operational problem before a technical one.
  • Good workflows reduce ambiguity and increase confidence.
