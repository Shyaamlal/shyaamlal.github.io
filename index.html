Themes I keep returning to

  • Human oversight becomes more important, not less.
  • Traceability matters more when systems become probabilistic.
  • AI works best when embedded into existing workflows.
  • Reliability is often an operational problem before a technical one.
  • Good workflows reduce ambiguity and increase confidence.
